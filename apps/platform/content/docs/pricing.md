## Payment & Billing

- Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- ACH bank transfer (Team tier and above)
- Wire transfer (Enterprise only)
- Purchase orders (Enterprise only)

### Billing Cycle
- Monthly: Billed on signup date each month
- Annual: Billed once per year (20% discount)

### Tax
- Prices shown exclude VAT/sales tax
- Tax calculated at checkout based on location

### Refund Policy
- **14-day money-back guarantee** on first payment
- No questions asked refund within 14 days
- After 14 days: Pro-rated refunds on annual plans

### Upgrade/Downgrade
- **Upgrade anytime** - Pro-rated charge immediately
- **Downgrade anytime** - Pro-rated credit applies to next bill
- No penalties for changing plans

### Cancellation
- Cancel anytime from account settings
- Access continues until end of current billing period
- Export your data before canceling
- Can reactivate later with same data (if within 90 days)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I try before I buy?

Yes. Start with the **Free tier** (no credit card required). Upgrade to Pro or Team when ready.

### What happens if I exceed my AI request limit?

You'll get a notification at 80% usage. Options:
1. Purchase additional AI request add-on
2. Upgrade to higher tier
3. Wait until next billing cycle (resets monthly)

AI requests don't roll over month-to-month.

### Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Pro-rated charges/credits applied automatically.

### Do you offer discounts?

- **Annual billing:** 20% off all plans
- **Non-profits:** 50% off Team tier (contact us)
- **Education:** 50% off Team tier (verified educators)
- **Startups:** Potential credits (apply to startup program)

### What if my team grows beyond 20 people?

**Team tier:** Add members at $10/month per additional user
**Or upgrade to Enterprise** for unlimited members

### Can I export my data if I cancel?

Yes. Export anytime in Yarn Spinner, JSON, or CSV format. Your data is yours.

### Is there a free trial for Pro or Team?

**14-day free trial** available for Pro and Team tiers. No credit card required for trial.

### What's included in "support"?

- **Free tier:** Community Discord, documentation
- **Pro tier:** Email support (48-hour response)
- **Team tier:** Priority email (24-hour response)
- **Enterprise:** Dedicated support team, SLA, Slack/Teams channel

### Can I get an invoice for my company?

Yes. Invoices automatically generated and emailed. Available in billing dashboard.

### Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes, for Enterprise tier. Contact sales for custom pricing based on team size and usage.

### Can we negotiate Enterprise pricing?

Yes. Enterprise pricing is flexible based on:
- Team size
- Usage requirements
- Contract length
- Custom features needed

[Contact sales](/contact?plan=enterprise) to discuss.
