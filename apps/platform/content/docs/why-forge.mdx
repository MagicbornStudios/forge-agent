# Why Use Forge?

**Build narrative-driven projects in days, not months**

Forge is to dialogue and narrative what the Unity Asset Store is to game development. We provide the meta-content layer that sits above your game engine, handling story structure, character relationships, dialogue systems, and narrative context so you can focus on your unique creative vision.

---

## The Problem: Creative Teams Move Too Slowly

Building custom narrative tools is a massive time sink that pulls developers away from their actual game or interactive experience:

- **6+ months** to build a custom dialogue editor from scratch
- **No AI integration** - you build everything yourself
- **Context continuity nightmares** - tracking story state across branches is manual
- **No progress visibility** - teams struggle to answer "how far along are we?"
- **Maintenance burden** - every custom tool needs ongoing support

### What Teams Struggle With

Creative projects lack the natural structure that software projects have. In code, you have files, functions, and tests. In narrative work, you have:

- **Direction without structure** - "Act 2" means different things to different people
- **Progress tracking** - How do you measure story completion?
- **Context continuity** - Did the player already meet this character? In what context?
- **Team coordination** - Writers, designers, and developers need shared language
- **Branching complexity** - Storylets and choice trees grow exponentially

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Forge a game engine?

No. Forge handles the narrative/dialogue/character layer. You still use Unity, Unreal, Godot, or custom engines for gameplay, rendering, and physics. Think of Forge as complementary to game engines.

### Can I use my own AI models?

Yes. Forge integrates with OpenRouter for multi-model routing, but you can bring your own API keys or use local models.

### What if I need to migrate away?

Export your data anytime in JSON or Yarn Spinner format. No lock-in. Your content is yours.

### How does the Developer Program work?

Build custom editors with our dev-kit, submit for review, earn 70% of sales when other teams buy your editor. Like the Unity Asset Store for narrative tools.

### Do you support languages other than English?

Yes. Forge is internationalization-ready. Your content can be in any language, and AI assistants support 50+ languages.
